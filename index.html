FAQ

Questions Before You Enter the Hall.

Is VikingPal free to use?

Yes. The Wanderer path is free and includes 15 starter runes, the full Hall of Gods, all fifteen reflection doors, your ritual journal, and hall lore. The app is ad-supported until you unlock Hrafnlaus or Ragnarök.

What is a rune?

A rune is the currency of the hall. Each time you consult a god or open a reflection door, one rune is spent. You start with 15 free runes and can buy more any time — or claim 300 runes with the Ragnarök purchase.

Who are the nine gods?

Odin, Thor, Loki, Freya, Tyr, Frigg, Balder, Heimdall, and Njord. Each speaks in their own voice — oracular, thundering, mischievous, amber-warm, iron-honest, calm, light-clear, watchful, or tidal. Enter the sanctum of the one whose wisdom fits your question.

What are the 15 reflection doors?

Daily hall guidance, trust and inner strength, ritual reflection, hope in heavy weather, forgiveness, love and compassion, strength and courage, gratitude, purpose and calling, family and relationships, peace and restless mind, skald wisdom, humility and character, overcoming temptation, and evening hall close. Each door costs one rune.

What is the difference between Hrafnlaus and Ragnarök?

Hrafnlaus is a one-time purchase that permanently removes every ad from the app. Ragnarök includes everything in Hrafnlaus — ad-free forever — plus 300 runes added to your vault and the cinematic Ragnarök theme. Both are one-time purchases with no subscription.

What does the Ragnarök purchase include?

Ragnarök is a one-time purchase that permanently removes every ad from the app, adds 300 runes to your vault, and unlocks the cinematic Ragnarök theme — a living fire-world with unique atmosphere on every screen. No subscription. Restores across your devices.

Does VikingPal have subscriptions?

No. VikingPal does not sell subscriptions. You can wander for free with ads, unlock Hrafnlaus or Ragnarök once to remove ads (and claim runes with Ragnarök), or buy rune top-ups whenever you need more.
