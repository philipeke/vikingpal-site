04

Your Spiritual Content

Your ritual journal, reflections, and personal entries are yours alone. We treat this content with the highest level of privacy. It is stored securely and never used for advertising, profiling, or sold to third parties under any circumstances.

When you use Ask VikingPal (Premium), your question is sent to our AI service provider to generate a response. We do not associate these questions with advertising profiles. Your questions are not stored in identifiable form beyond what is necessary to deliver the response.

05

Third Parties & Data Processors

We use the following trusted service providers to operate VikingPal:

  • Google Firebase — authentication, database, cloud functions, and analytics (Google LLC, EU Standard Contractual Clauses applied)
  • Apple App Store / Google Play — app distribution and in-app purchase processing
  • AI service provider — processing Ask VikingPal queries (Premium only; anonymised where possible)
  • Crash reporting — anonymised diagnostic data to improve app stability

We do not share your data with advertisers or data brokers.

07

Security

We implement technical and organisational measures to protect your data:

  • Data in transit encrypted with TLS 1.2+
  • Data at rest encrypted via Firebase Security Rules and Google Cloud encryption
  • Authentication via secure tokens (no plain-text passwords stored)
  • Access to production data restricted to essential personnel only
  • Regular security reviews and dependency updates

No system is 100% secure. If a breach occurs that affects your rights, we will notify you and the relevant supervisory authority as required by GDPR Article 33–34.

08

Your Rights Under GDPR

As a data subject, you have the following rights under the GDPR:

Art. 15
Right of Access

Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.

Art. 16
Right to Rectification

Correct inaccurate or incomplete personal data.

Art. 17
Right to Erasure

Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten").

Art. 18
Right to Restriction

Restrict processing of your data in certain circumstances.

Art. 20
Right to Portability

Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.

Art. 21
Right to Object

Object to processing based on legitimate interest.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@oakdev.app. We will respond within 30 days. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Swedish Data Protection Authority (IMY) at imy.se.

09

Children

VikingPal is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us at hello@oakdev.app and we will delete it promptly.
