Delete Your Account

You have the right to permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time — no questions asked.

This action is permanent and cannot be undone. Deleting your account will permanently erase all your data, your purchase entitlements, your rune balance, your ritual journal, and your reflection history. There is no grace period — once confirmed in the app, deletion begins immediately.

Delete in the App
  1. 1Open the VikingPal app on your device.
  2. 2Go to Settings (profile or menu area).

  4. 4Tap Delete Account.
  5. 5Confirm the deletion. Your account and all data are permanently erased.
