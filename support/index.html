Frequently Asked Questions

Quick Answers

Everything you need to know about VikingPal. Can't find your answer? Send us a message.

Is VikingPal free to use?

Yes. The Grace tier is included with the app at no cost. It gives you full Bible access, daily reflection, prayer tracking, 15 reflection paths, Selah, Let Go, and core study tools. Premium unlocks Ask VikingPal, Connected Features, and deeper personalization.

What is Ask VikingPal?

Ask VikingPal is a Premium AI guidance feature. Bring your real questions — struggles, decisions, doubts, or praise — and receive answers shaped by Scripture and Christian conviction, crafted personally for your situation.

What are the 15 reflection paths?

Daily guidance, faith and trust, prayer reflection, patience and hope, forgiveness, mercy and compassion, faith and strength, grateful heart, life purpose, family, inner peace, wisdom, good character, resisting temptation, and evening rest.

How do I cancel my Premium subscription?

You can cancel Premium at any time through your device subscription settings. On iPhone/iPad: Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → VikingPal. On Android: Google Play → Account → Subscriptions → VikingPal. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period.

How do I delete my account?

You can delete your account in the app (Settings → scroll down → Delete Account) or by emailing us at hello@oakdev.app with the subject "Delete Account." See our Delete Account page for full instructions.

What languages does VikingPal support?

VikingPal supports more than 100 locale options. The app is built to travel with believers in many different settings, cultures, and languages while remaining clearly rooted in Protestant Christian conviction.

Is VikingPal theologically neutral?

No. VikingPal is rooted in Protestant Christian conviction and treats Scripture as the final authority. It is not denominationally specific, but its content is consistently Protestant and Bible-centred. It is not designed for Catholic, Orthodox, or non-Christian users.

I found a bug. How do I report it?

Please email us at hello@oakdev.app with details about the issue — your device type, OS version, app version, and a description of what happened. Screenshots are very helpful. We aim to respond to bug reports within 3 business days.
